## P — Problem

Fasting apps optimise for engagement, and engagement mechanics quietly work against the
thing fasting is supposed to give you. Streaks turn a practice into a debt: miss a day and
the app tells you you're "broken," so users either fake it or churn in shame. Endless
configuration ("pick your protocol, set your window, tune your macros") loads the daily
decision back onto the user's willpower — the opposite of restful. And most apps treat
food-restriction as a pure biohack, stripped of any older framework that gave it meaning
or a built-in schedule.

Meanwhile there's an existing rhythm — the Hindu lunar calendar — that has scheduled fasts
(Ekadashi, Purnima, Amavasya, Pradosh) for over a thousand years, decided by the moon
rather than the user. It's precise, recurring, and culturally grounded, but it isn't
packaged for people who want an honest, modern, non-medical way to keep it.

## S — Solution

Soma schedules fasts on lunar days and frames the goal as **rhythm, not streaks**. The
mechanics enforce the framing:

- **The moon picks the days.** A 60-day schedule of Ekadashi, full/new moon, Pradosh,
  Sankashti, and Shivaratri is generated from lunar math — the user doesn't decide *when*,
  removing the daily willpower tax.
- **Non-punitive by construction.** The 40-day **Mandala Engine** tracks a
  `completionRate` against expected days but is *never* allowed a "broken" state
  (`MandalaStatus` ∈ in-progress / completed / partial). A source-level invariant test
  (`__tests__/no-streak.test.ts`) fails the build if the word "streak" ever appears in
  user-facing copy.
- **Practice over metric.** Each fast pairs with a 10-minute meditation and captures
  optional **before/after subjective logs** (energy, focus, mood, sleep) that roll up into
  **Personal Deltas** — the user's own evidence, not a leaderboard.
- **Honest about what it is.** Two-layer copy (`plain` → `tradition` / `science`) lets each
  reader meet the day on their terms, and a persistent **wellness-not-medicine**
  disclaimer plus a hard **safety gate** (under-18, pregnancy, disordered-eating, diabetes
  are blocked) keep the product from overclaiming.

## A — Audience

Adults (18+, per the safety gate) who want a fasting practice with a spine but without the
guilt machinery. Segmented by the intent picker into four working personas — see
[Personas](./2026-07-05-personas.md):

- **The Optimizer** — wants the mechanism and the deltas (performance / scientific).
- **The Tradition-Keeper** — keeps the vrat properly, cares about sunrise-accurate timing
  (devotional / traditional).
- **The App-Fatigued Minimalist** — churned from streak apps, wants less choice
  (minimal / coach). *The primary beneficiary of the current consolidation.*
- **The Curious Newcomer** — new to fasting, needs gentle, plain-language guidance
  (performance / coach).

Explicitly **out of audience:** anyone the safety gate blocks. These are honest refusals,
not a funnel to widen.

## K — Key metrics

Proposed. One primary per stage; guardrails must not regress. All are computable from data
the app already holds (local-first) — instrumentation plan needed before any are trusted.

### Activation (does a new user reach first value?)
- **Primary — First fast started.** % of users who begin their first fast within 7 days of
  onboarding. First value is *doing a fast*, not finishing onboarding.
- **Onboarding completion rate.** % who finish the flow (reach `completeOnboarding`).
  Expected to *rise* with the 8 → ~5 step trim (audit F3). Segment by intent — watch that
  Tradition-Keeper location-set rate and Newcomer completion don't fall.

### Rhythm adherence (are they keeping the practice?)
- **Primary — Mandala completion rate.** The app's own `completionRate` per 40-day mandala
  (observed ÷ expected days; `threshold` 0.6, `minExpected` 3). Report the *distribution*,
  never a streak. This is the north-star adherence signal.
- **Fasts observed per lunar month** vs. scheduled — a simpler adherence proxy.

### Delta improvements (is the practice doing something for them?)
- **Primary — Positive Personal Deltas surfaced.** % of active users with at least one
  computed `PersonalDelta` (focus / energy / mood / sleep) above noise, from before/after
  logs. Ties directly to the Optimizer's reason to stay.
- **Subjective-log completion rate.** % of fasts with both a pre-log and post-log — the
  input that makes deltas possible.

### Retention proxies (do they come back?)
- **Primary — Return-by-next-lunar-day.** % of users who start a fast on their *next*
  scheduled lunar day after their first. Retention that matches the product's own cadence
  beats generic DAU/WAU.
- **Multi-mandala continuation.** % who reach a second 40-day mandala.

### Guardrails (must not regress)
- **Safety-gate integrity.** Blocked-flag users must remain 100% blocked from starting a
  fast. Non-negotiable — see Personas §5.
- **No-streak invariant.** `no-streak.test.ts` stays green; no engagement metric is ever
  reframed as a streak in the UI.
- **Tithi accuracy.** Share of Tradition-Keeper (tradition-intent) sessions computed at
  `sunrise` accuracy should not drop after location leaves the required onboarding path.
