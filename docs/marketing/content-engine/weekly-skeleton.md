# Weekly Skeleton — Soma

> The fixed slot grid. Ported from `yirifi-social-marketing/content-engine/calendar/weekly-skeleton.md`, sized for a solo cadence. No slot = no ship: volume is bounded by the grid, not by mood.

**Refresh:** Friday plan locks next week. **Times:** local, evening skew (spiritual/wellness audience engages evenings + early morning).

---

## The grid (solo baseline — ~5 posts/week)

`IG` = Instagram feed · `IG-R` = Reel · `TT` = TikTok · `X` = X/Twitter · `ST` = Stories

| Day | Slot | Channel | Series | Archetype | Pillar |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | 08:00 | IG carousel | Lunar Fasting 101 | blog-led | P1 |
| Mon | 18:00 | X thread | Lunar Fasting 101 (atomized) | blog-led | P1 |
| **Wed** | 18:00 | IG-R + TT | Tradition & Meaning *or* Wellness | card-native / blog-led | P2 / P3 |
| **Fri** | 12:00 | IG carousel | Wellness & the Body | blog-led | P3 |
| **Any** | — | IG-R + ST + X | **This Month's Sky** (date-triggered) | card-native | P4 |
| **Any** | — | X single / ST | Building in the Open | card-native | P5 |

**Date-triggered (P4) is the engine's heartbeat.** The lunar calendar fires ~6 posts/month with no ideation cost: Ekadashi (×2), Pradosh (×2), Purnima (×1), Amavasya (×1). Post the day *before* each fast — Story + Reel + tweet — so followers can prepare. These displace nothing; they layer on.

## Daily rhythm within a slot

- **1 anchor/week** (a blog post) → atomized into the Mon carousel + Mon X thread + one Reel.
- **Stories daily-ish** — low-effort: tonight's moon, a poll ("fasting today?"), a re-share of a comment.
- **Reply window:** first 2 hours after any feed post — comments are the second post.

## Pillar-ratio target (rolling 4 weeks)

| Pillar | Typical slots/wk | Target % |
|---|---|---|
| P1 Lunar Fasting 101 | 2 (Mon carousel + X) | 30% |
| P2 Tradition & Meaning | 1 (Wed alt) | 25% |
| P3 Wellness & the Body | 1–2 (Fri carousel) | 20% |
| P4 This Month's Sky | date-triggered (~1.5/wk) | 20% |
| P5 Building in the Open | opportunistic | 5% |

If the mix drifts >10% off target over 4 weeks, rebalance next Friday's pick.

## Empty-slot policy

No winning pick + no evergreen fallback → **skip the slot**. A skipped slot beats a filler post. Evergreen fallbacks live in the blog archive (re-atomize an old post 3–6 months later).

## Scaling up

When Soma has help / leaves beta: add a Sat "Tithi Tonight" card-native slot, a light weekly email (Recipe D), and a second founder slot. Then graduate the sensing layer toward Yirifi's `signals/` subsystem.
