## The 4 Content Pillars

Each pillar owns a keyword territory, maps to a stage of the buyer journey, and feeds a cluster of repeatable posts.

### Pillar 1 — Lunar Fasting 101 _(Searchable · Awareness)_
Educational evergreen that captures existing search demand. This is the SEO backbone.

- **Owns:** `what is lunar fasting`, `what is Ekadashi`, `what is a tithi`, `Purnima meaning`, `Amavasya fast`, `Pradosh vrat`, `how to do Ekadashi fasting`
- **Content type:** hub-and-spoke (one "Lunar Fasting: Complete Guide" hub → a spoke per fast/term)
- **Product tie-in:** every piece ends with "Soma computes tonight's tithi for you."
- **Example posts:** "What is Ekadashi and why is it kept without grains?" · "The four main Hindu fasts, explained" · "Tithi vs. calendar day: why the moon sets the date"

### Pillar 2 — The Tradition & Meaning _(Shareable · Brand)_
Story and heritage. This is what makes Soma *feel* like something, not a utility. Drives shares and brand affinity.

- **Owns:** the emotional narrative — Sanatan Dharma, why we fast with the moon, the myth of Soma / Chandra, wisdom passed parent-to-child
- **Content type:** thought leadership, storytelling, mini-essays
- **Product tie-in:** soft — "we carry this rhythm forward."
- **Example posts:** "Before calendars, the sky told you when to fast" · "Why the month runs with the moon" · "Soma: the moon, the nectar, the name behind the app"

### Pillar 3 — Wellness & the Body _(Searchable + Shareable · Consideration)_
Bridges tradition to a modern wellness audience — the crowd searching fasting/wellness who don't yet know lunar fasting exists.

- **Owns:** `fasting benefits`, `mindful fasting`, `intermittent fasting vs lunar fasting`, `fasting and rest`, `circadian / lunar rhythm`, `spiritual wellness practice`
- **Content type:** use-case content ([persona] + [use-case]), comparison ("lunar fasting vs intermittent fasting"), data/science-meets-tradition
- **Product tie-in:** "a gentler, meaning-first way to fast."
- **Example posts:** "Lunar fasting vs. intermittent fasting: what's the difference?" · "Fasting as rest, not restriction" · "A spiritual wellness practice that runs on autopilot"

### Pillar 4 — This Month's Sky _(Timely · Recurring · Retention)_
The engine that guarantees weekly cadence. Tied to the **real lunar calendar**, so the calendar itself tells you what to post and when.

- **Owns:** `next Ekadashi date`, `Purnima [month] 2026`, `Amavasya date`, `tonight's tithi`, `[month] fasting calendar`
- **Content type:** recurring templated posts — high intent, easy to produce
- **Product tie-in:** direct — "open Soma to keep the fast."
- **Example posts:** "🌘 Ekadashi is tomorrow — here's how to keep it" · "This month's fasting calendar" · "Tonight's tithi: [X]"
- **Natural triggers:** Ekadashi (2×/month), Purnima (1×), Amavasya (1×), Pradosh (2×) = ~6 guaranteed date-anchored posts every month.

> Optional 5th pillar — **Building Soma in the open** (behind-the-scenes / founder journey / product updates). Keep to ~10–15% of the mix; great for X and for the launch-out-of-beta moment.

## Weekly Posting Plan

**Cadence:** 1 pillar "anchor" idea per week, repurposed across platforms, plus date-anchored Pillar-4 posts whenever the lunar calendar triggers one. Batch in one ~2-hour session.

**Platforms (by fit for a visual, lifestyle, spiritual brand):** Instagram (primary — Reels + carousels), TikTok (reach), X/Twitter (build-in-public + community). Add Pinterest later — moon/phase visuals are evergreen search there.

### Weekly template

| Day | Instagram | TikTok | X / Twitter |
|-----|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Mon | Carousel — Pillar 1 (educational) | — | Thread — unpack the same topic |
| Tue | Story — poll / question | — | Engagement (reply in niche) |
| Wed | Reel — Pillar 2 or 3 (story/wellness) | Repost the Reel | 1 short insight |
| Thu | — | — | Build-in-public note (Pillar 5) |
| Fri | Reel or carousel — Pillar 4 if a fast is near | Repost | Hot take / question |
| Any | **Date-triggered:** Ekadashi/Purnima/Amavasya post → Story + Reel + tweet, day before the fast |

### The repurposing loop (1 anchor → a week of posts)
1. Write one pillar piece (a short essay, guide section, or script).
2. Extract 5–10 "content atoms" — a quotable line, one fast's rules, a myth, a stat, a before/after.
3. Adapt per platform: IG carousel (main points) · Reel (one atom, 20–40s, captioned) · X thread (takeaways) · Story (poll).
4. Each caption stands alone — never assume they saw the rest.
5. Schedule across the week; leave room for live replies.

### Hooks tuned to Soma
- **Curiosity:** "You've been told when to eat. The moon told your grandparents when to fast."
- **Value:** "The 4 Hindu fasts, and exactly what each one asks of you 👇"
- **Contrarian:** "Fasting isn't about restriction. It's about rhythm."
- **Timely:** "🌘 Ekadashi is tomorrow. Here's the one rule that matters."
- **Story:** "Before there were apps, there was the sky."

### First 4 weeks (starter calendar)
- **Week 1** — Pillar 1: "The four main fasts, explained" (carousel + thread) → CTA: open the app.
- **Week 2** — Pillar 2: "Why the month runs with the moon" (Reel narrative).
- **Week 3** — Pillar 3: "Lunar fasting vs. intermittent fasting" (carousel; bridges wellness audience).
- **Week 4** — Pillar 4: nearest Ekadashi/Purnima — "how to keep it" (Reel + Story + tweet).
- **Ongoing** — every real fast day = a date-anchored post; every product step = a build-in-public note.

### What to measure
- **Awareness:** reach, follower growth, saves (saves signal evergreen value — key for Pillars 1 & 4).
- **Engagement:** comments > likes; shares on Pillar 2.
- **Conversion:** profile → `/app/` clicks; beta sign-ups; branded searches for "Soma app".
- Weekly: note top 3 / bottom 3 posts and why; double down on formats that get **saves and shares**.
